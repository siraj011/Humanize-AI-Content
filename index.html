Frequently Asked Questions

How do you humanize AI content?

I completely rewrite the AI-generated content using natural language patterns, varied sentence structures, and human-like phrasing. This includes adding personal anecdotes, conversational elements, and ensuring the text flows naturally while maintaining the original meaning.

Can you guarantee my content will pass AI detection?

Yes, I guarantee that the content I humanize will pass all major AI detection tools (Originality.ai, GPTZero, etc.). If any content fails detection, I'll revise it for free until it passes.

What's your turnaround time?

Standard delivery is 24-48 hours depending on the order size, but I offer expedited delivery options (12 hours or less) for urgent projects at a small additional fee.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, all packages include free revisions. The number of revisions depends on the package you choose (Basic: 1 revision, Standard: 2 revisions, Premium: unlimited revisions).

What file formats do you accept?

I accept all common file formats including Word (.docx), Google Docs, plain text (.txt), and PDF. You can also simply paste your content in the order message if you prefer.
